# Power-Bi-dashboard-project
# R.G Synthetic Beverage Sales Dashboard

# Project Overview:-
The R.G Synthetic Beverage Sales Dashboard is a Power BI project that provides insights into the sales performance of R.G Synthetic Beverages. This interactive dashboard helps analyze sales trends, identify   top-performing products, monitor regional sales, and track profit margins to support data-driven business decisions.

# Features:-

->Total Sales Analysis – Monitor overall sales performance over time.

->Top & Bottom Products – Identify best-selling and low-performing products.

->Regional Sales Trends – Visualize sales distribution across different regions.

->Profit & Profit Margin Analysis – Track profitability for each product and region.

->Interactive Filters – Filter data by product, region, and time period for detailed analysis.

# Data Source:-

The dashboard uses a structured sales dataset containing:-

                                                        ->Product Name

                                                        ->Region

                                                        ->Quantity Sold

                                                        ->Unit Price

                                                        ->Cost

                                                        ->Revenue

                                                        ->Profit
                                                        
                                                        ->Sales

# Visualizations:-

->Clustered Bar Chart – Shows top and bottom products by sales or profit.

->Clustered Column Chart –it Shows profit margin by Category

->Line Chart – Displays sales trends over Day

->Funnel – Total sales & region by Product

->Pie/Donut Chart – Shows Revenue contribution by Category.

->Cards & KPI Tiles – Highlight total sales, total profit, total cost,total revenue and average sales

# Tools Used:-

Power BI Desktop – For building the dashboard and visualizations.

Excel – For storing and managing the dataset.

# Why This Project

This project was created to demonstrate how data analytics and visualization using Power BI can solve real-world business problems in the sales domain.

The R.G Synthetic Beverage Sales Dashboard helps convert raw sales data into meaningful insights that support strategic decision-making. Instead of relying on manual reports or spreadsheets, this dashboard provides a centralized, interactive, and visual view of business performance.

# Business Impact:-

->Data-Driven Decision Making: The dashboard provides management with real-time insights into sales performance, helping them make informed business decisions.

->Revenue Optimization: By identifying top-selling products and regions, resources can be allocated effectively to maximize revenue.

->Cost and Profit Management: Profit margin tracking helps in recognizing high-cost products and taking actions to improve profitability.

->Performance Monitoring: Continuous monitoring of sales trends and regional performance ensures timely interventions and strategic planning.

->Market Strategy: Enables identification of underperforming products and regions, guiding marketing and promotional efforts.
